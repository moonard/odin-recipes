Tripitas Tacos/Tacos de Tripas Recipe

Description

Simple and delicious tripas tacos!

Cook the tripas for longer for extra crunch!!

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds tripas
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bunch cilantro

Directions

  1. Fill pot with water over medium-high heat
  2. Thoroughly clean and rinse tripe quickly to avoid rubbery texture
  3. Simmer tripitas in pot for 30 minutes over low heat
  4. Allow to cool then remove fat in the broth
  5. Drain, dry, then cut into bite-size pieces
  6. Add oil to skillet and fry tripas for 8 to 10 minutes
  7. Serve with corn tortillas with onion, cilantro, and salsa
  8. Salt to taste
