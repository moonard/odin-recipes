Doenjang Jjigae Recipe

Description

Soul-warming Korean soybean paste soup!

It'll remind you of warm kitchens in cold winters, hearing your grandma cook while watching cartoons.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium potato
  • 1 small zucchini
  • 1 green Korean chili pepper
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 4 large shrimp
  • 2 1/2 cups of water
  • 7 dried anchovies
  • 5 tablesponns fermented soybean paste
  • 6 ounces medium-firm tofu
  • 2 green onions

Directions

  1. Peel and chop potatoes into small cubes
  2. Chop onions, green onions, and zucchinis into small cubes
  3. Mince garlic
  4. Stem and chop chili peppers
  5. Shell, devein, wash, and chop shrimp
  6. Combine potato, onion, zucchini, chili pepper, garlic, and shrimp into an earthenware pot
  7. Wrap gutted and dried anchovies in a dashi bag and add to pot
  8. Add water, cook over medium-high heat for 15 minutes or until boiling
  9. Stir in soybean paste and cook for another 20 minutes
  10. Add tofu and cook for another 3 minutes
  11. Remove and discard anchovy pouch
  12. Sprinkle in green onions to taste
