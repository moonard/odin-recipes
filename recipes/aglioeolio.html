Aglio e Olio Recipe

Description

A delicious oil-based pasta garnished with parsley. Perfect for hot summer days.

For protein, consider adding shrimp!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1/4 cup Italian parsley

Directions

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Cook spaghetti al dente
  3. Thinly slice or mince garlic
  4. Combine olive oil, garlic, and red pepper into a separate pan
  5. Cook until garlic is golden brown then remove pan from heat
  6. Save half a cup of pasta water then drain spaghetti
  7. Place pasta water and spaghetti into the pan
  8. Mix well while adding salt and black pepper to taste
  9. Mix in parsley and serve
